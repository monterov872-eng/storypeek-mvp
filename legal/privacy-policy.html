Privacy Policy for Silent View

Effective date: June 18, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Montavia LLC ("Montavia," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information in connection with the Silent View mobile application for Android (the "App"). By downloading, accessing, or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the App.

Silent View is an independent application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or connected to Instagram, Meta Platforms, Inc., or any of their subsidiaries or affiliates. The App provides access only to content that is already publicly available.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to information processed through the App. It does not apply to third-party services, websites, networks, or content that we do not own or control, even if accessed through or linked from the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Search terms, usernames, or queries you enter into the App to locate publicly available content.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device information — device model, operating system and version, language, region/country settings, screen attributes, and similar technical data.
  • Identifiers — a randomly generated installation/device identifier used for app functionality (such as rate limiting and session validation), and a mobile advertising identifier (e.g., Google Advertising ID) used for advertising.
  • Network information — IP address and general (coarse) location inferred from it, and connection information.
  • Log and crash data — diagnostic logs, error reports, crash logs, and stack traces used to maintain and improve stability.
  • Usage and analytics data — features used, screens viewed, interactions, session duration, and similar analytics.

2.3 Information from third parties

  • Advertising and analytics partners (such as Google) may provide us with aggregated or limited information related to ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention.

We do not ask for, collect, or store Instagram (or other third-party platform) passwords or login credentials, and the App does not require you to log in to any third-party platform.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob, including banner ads and rewarded ads. To serve, measure, and improve ads, Google and its partners may collect and process device identifiers (including the advertising identifier), IP address, and ad-interaction data, and may use this information for personalized or non-personalized advertising in accordance with their own policies.

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
  • AdMob & Google's advertising policies: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

Where required by law, the App requests applicable consent before serving personalized ads and otherwise requests non-personalized ads. You can manage or reset your advertising identifier, or opt out of ad personalization, in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

4. Analytics and Crash Reporting

We may use analytics and crash-reporting tools to understand usage, diagnose problems, and improve the App. These tools may process device information, identifiers, and event/usage data on our behalf.

5. How We Use Information

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and secure the App and its features;
  • Locate and display publicly available content you request;
  • Display and measure advertisements, including rewarded and banner ads;
  • Detect, prevent, and address abuse, fraud, security issues, and violations of our Terms;
  • Apply rate limits and validate sessions;
  • Diagnose crashes and analyze performance and usage;
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.

6. Legal Bases for Processing (EEA/UK)

Where the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation applies, we process personal data on the following legal bases: (a) performance of a contract (providing the App); (b) our legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, analytics, and improvement of the App); (c) your consent (e.g., for personalized advertising where required); and (d) compliance with legal obligations.

7. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal data for money. We may share information:

  • With service providers and partners (such as advertising, analytics, hosting, and crash-reporting providers, including Google) that process information on our behalf or in accordance with their policies;
  • For legal reasons, including to comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, safety, or security of Montavia, our users, or others;
  • In connection with a business transaction, such as a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets;
  • With your consent or at your direction.

Some sharing for advertising purposes may be considered a "sale" or "sharing" of personal information under certain U.S. state privacy laws; see Section 11 for your choices.

8. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including providing the App, complying with legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing our agreements. Locally stored data (such as recent search history) remains on your device until you clear it (for example, by clearing app data or uninstalling the App). Server-side operational records (such as rate-limit and session data) are short-lived and expire automatically.

9. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information, including encryption of network traffic in transit. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. International Data Transfers
